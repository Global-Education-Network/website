Who We Are

About Us

Global Education Network (GEN) is a volunteer-driven initiative supporting underprivileged students in Pakistan. We connect sponsors from Norway and beyond with deserving students to ensure consistent financial and mentorship support.

Our initial focus is Pakistan, but we plan to expand our scope in the future.
